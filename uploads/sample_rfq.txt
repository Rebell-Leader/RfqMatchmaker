RFQ for High School Computer Class Equipment

RFQ Details:
Title: Supply and Delivery of Laptops and Monitors for High School Computer Lab
Description: Procurement of laptops and monitors for the new computer lab at Jefferson High School

Requesting Organization: Jefferson High School
Contact: procurement@jeffersonhs.edu
